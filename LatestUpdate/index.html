Gameplay

Fantasy One is a fully functioning fantasy football platform, powered by the blockchain that offers players the opportunity to play the game they love and also gives them better opportunities to earn rewards, in this game everyone's is a winner.  

Classic Mode

In Classic Mode, users enter a P2E fantasy league with grand prizes for high-place finishes at the end of each season.
Classic Mode offers both Public and Private Leagues to all users. Classic Mode is traditional fantasy football (38 GWs), except private Leagues are invitation-only and have a different reward structure.

Weekend League

Fantasy One will offer single Game week competitions called Weekend League.
Weekend League will stand alone, meaning users need to create a new team to the one being used in Classic Mode. Whether users select the same players or different players is personal choice. Weekend League will offer greater opportunity to earn $BALLER rewards, through both the Earnings Algorithm and grand prizes for high-place finishes.
Simply put, Weekend League works in the same way as Classic Mode, except it only applies to the upcoming round of Premier League fixtures. This gives players 38 more chances to win grand prizes, instead of just 1 chance in Classic Mode.

One Cup

Fantasy One will offer a third competition called One Cup. One Cup will be a special event in which users gain access to the greatest rewards offered by the platform. There is 1 One Cup every year, which will be organized for halfway through the Premier League season.
One Cups will last for a total of 4 weeks and will involve unique features that solely apply to this Game Mode.
Unique features will include randomized rewards and randomized penalties, offering a painstaking, high-octane version of fantasy football, in which a user’s management skills are constantly tested (or boosted) by factors that are out of their own control.

What Makes Fantasy One different?

Fantasy One is powered by the blockchain, so you get to enjoy fantasy football with added on features that makes the blockchain and the whole web3 space special.

Utility Token

As a Fantasy One user you have access to the utility token $FAN, $FAN is the native token of Fantasy One, which is used for swapping for $SQUAD tokens which are used for player selections, $FAN can also be traded on popular exchanges.

Token Rewards

Fantasy One offers token rewards to users after every game week, based on the performance of their squad selection, users will be automatically paid $BALLER tokens, which can be traded on popular exchanges. 

Unlimited Transfers

Fantasy One users can make unlimited transfers every game week without any penalties, this gives users more flexibility on squad selections. 

POTW

POTW is an NFT Collection based on real life players who perform exceptionally well on a game week, those players will be listed on the POTW Marketplace for users to purchase. 

POTW Marketplace

POTW Marketplace is an NFT Marketplace for the POTW Collection where users can trade POTW NFT's.

Private League Rewards

Users will have the opportunity to create a private league with family, friends and colleagues with token rewards in escrow.

About Us

While fantasy football is a casual gaming experience and allows users to compete on the leaderboard through the points they earn each week, a key drawback is lost engagement over the course of the season due to no real sense of progression for most players. In FPL, only five users earn prizes out of over 9 million in total, and the game has few notable mechanics that combat this reality. Despite this, FPL captures the majority of usership for fantasy football fans around the world, which reflects how popular the fundamentals of the game really are. However, with almost no incentives for playing and limited game mechanics, Fantasy One believes there is potential left on the table. Other platforms (beyond FPL), hosted by news outlets and sports betting platforms, do offer cash prizes for 1st place finishes, but Fantasy One can offer bigger grand prizes to a higher number of users in addition to passive earning potential for all players. This is enabled by the platform’s emphasis on liquidity generation and the Fantasy One Earnings Algorithm. Multiple game modes are another unique selling point for the Fantasy One platform. All other fantasy leagues offer basic gameplay, involving squad selection and a point scoring system for real world performances across the full 38-game Premier League season. Fantasy One offers reduced timeframe game modes with more dynamic game mechanics for a high octane playing experience, in addition to Classic Mode, which is offered with greater earning potential than any competitor.
